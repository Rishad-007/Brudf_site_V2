About BRUDF

The Begum Rokeya University Debate Forum (BRUDF) stands as a beacon of intellectual discourse and critical thinking at our esteemed institution. We are dedicated to fostering a culture of reasoned debate, articulate expression, and analytical thinking among our students. Our forum provides a platform where diverse perspectives meet, ideas are challenged, and minds are sharpened through the art of debate.
